About Smart-Tech

Jeff Odhiambo, a student from Jommo Kenyatta university of Agriculture and Technology, at First Year came up with a idea of creating a Smart School, that is capable of assising students who might be intrested in learning Technology related courses and are not able to afford the fees.
The name of his small organisation is Smart Tech as it not only offers education services but also alot of other IT services.
In His school, teachers can register with him but agrees to offer free classes before being allowed to proceed in teaching. His learning materials are also assesed to ensure that they are not misleading.
